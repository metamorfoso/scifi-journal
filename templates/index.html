About

Sponge is an online magazine for science fiction and speculative fiction, based in the post-apocalpytic city of Christchurch, New Zealand. We publish short stories, poetry, and reviews of science fiction from New Zealand and Pacific writers, with a particular focus on new and emerging writers.

The magazine will soon be available to read online or to download as a pdf, mobi, or epub. We also plan to make an audio version of our magazine so if you are accepted to Sponge we will ask you to record a reading of your story. These will later be available for free on Band Camp.

Sponge is currently open for submissions!

We are looking for weird and wonderful stories for our first issue, and artwork for the cover.

Please submit your stories, poetry, or reviews through the submissions page on the website. Information about manuscript formating and other submission guidelines can be found on this page.

If you have any questions please contact editor@sponge.nz

Special thanks to Ivan Ignatov for designing and building this website and to Ben Uffindell for coming up with the name.
