About

Sponge is an online journal for science fiction and speculative fiction, based in the post-apocalpytic city of Christchurch, New Zealand. We publish short stories, poetry, and reviews of science fiction from New Zealand and Pacific writers, with a particular focus on new and emerging writers.

Our first issue is finally here! You can read it online or download it as a PDF, MOBI, or EPUB. We also plan to release an audio version of the issue (voiced by our talented authors). This will be soon available on Band Camp.
